Balamurugan V Raman (Bala) is a Senior Software Engineer working out of Nuernberg, Germany.

Currently, he works for adidas AG in their headquarters focusing on building an amazing suite of services used in adidas retail stores. His interests include all things technical and building and running effective systems and processes. In the past, he worked for BlippAR India focusing on building ad-hoc computer vision services and maintaining AR platform of BlippAR and LayAR. Previously, as part of Dreamworks Dedicated Unit (Dreamworks Animation Projects Unit) of Technicolor, India worked on developing interfacing tools for renderfarm, data pipelines and monitoring solutions. Before that, he worked for Manhattan Associates developing Warehouse Management Systems and United Online in email anti-spam systems.

His interests include Distributed Systems, Machine Learning and Functional Programming.
